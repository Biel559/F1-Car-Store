Our Story

Founded in 2005, F1 Car Store began as a passion project by former Formula 1 engineer Gabriel Campopiano Rosa. After years of working with top teams, Gabriel wanted to bring the thrill of Formula 1 to enthusiasts worldwide by offering authentic, race-ready vehicles.

What started as a small garage in Monaco has now grown into the world's premier destination for Formula 1 cars. We work directly with all 10 current F1 teams to source the most exclusive vehicles, from current season models to historic championship-winning cars.

Our mission is to preserve the engineering excellence of Formula 1 while making these incredible machines accessible to collectors and racing enthusiasts. Each vehicle in our inventory undergoes rigorous inspection and comes with full documentation of its racing history.
